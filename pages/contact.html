Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a flight?

To book a flight, simply visit our homepage, enter your departure and arrival cities, select your travel dates, and click "Search Flights". Choose your preferred flight and follow the booking process. You'll need to provide passenger details and payment information.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, you can manage your bookings through your account. Most bookings can be modified up to 24 hours before departure. Cancellations are subject to the airline's cancellation policy, and you may receive a refund or travel credit.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are secured with SSL encryption.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive travel insurance options during the booking process. This includes trip cancellation, medical coverage, and baggage protection.

How do I earn loyalty rewards?

Every booking you make earns you loyalty points. These points can be redeemed for discounts on future bookings, free flights, or hotel upgrades. Members also get access to exclusive deals.

What is your refund policy?

Refund policies vary depending on your booking type and the supplier's terms. Most refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. Non-refundable bookings may receive travel credits instead.
